FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Dial *805# from your ethio telecom number to instantly check your remaining data, SMS, and voice balance. You can also check via the my.et app or by sending "bal" to 805.

Dial *900# to browse and subscribe to available data packages. You can also activate plans through the my.et app, Telebirr, or by purchasing a voucher from any ethio telecom shop.

Telebirr is Ethiopia's leading mobile money service. To register, dial *127# and follow the prompts. You'll need your ID number and a passport photo. Registration is free and takes less than 5 minutes.

5G is available in 12 cities including Addis Ababa, Dire Dawa, and Bahir Dar. You need a 5G-compatible phone and a 5G plan. Visit any ethio telecom shop to upgrade your SIM and subscribe to a 5G data plan.

Dial *127# → select "Transfer Money" → enter recipient number and amount → confirm with your PIN. You can also use the Telebirr app for a more visual experience. Transfers to other Telebirr users are free.

Visit the nearest ethio telecom branch with your ID. Report the loss and request a SIM replacement. Your number will be preserved. For Telebirr users, also call 805 to temporarily block your account for security.
